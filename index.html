Choose your gamemode

Singleplayer In this mode, the player plays alone and must find as many words as possible by using the last letter of the previous word. There is no time limit and the player can continue as long as he/she can find new words.

Hot-Seat (2-5 players) In this mode, a maximum of five players take turns playing. Each player must find a word within a certain time limit by using the last letter of the previous word. The player who cannot find a word or repeats a word will be eliminated, and the last player remaining wins.

Multiplayer (2-5 players) This is an online mode where multiple players take turns coming up with a word that starts with the last letter of the previous word that was said. The goal is to continue the game until there is only one player left, who is the winner.
